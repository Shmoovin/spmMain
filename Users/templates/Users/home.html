This application provides a simple way to organize and coordinate group efforts for Oakland University CSI students who are completing their Sophomore projects.


How to use SoftProjectManager:
If you need to get back to this set of instructions, click on SoftProjectManager!
  1. Go to Register to create an account and fill out the form to sign up
  2. Login to your newly created account
  3. If your group has not been created yet, click on Create Group and fill out the form
  4. This will bring you to the Join Group tab, where you can join the group that you created
  5. Now on the main page it should show your group name, here you can click Create New Task to create a new task
  6. Fill out the form and then click create, the task should now appear on the main page
  7. To set a task as complete, click on the task name, click edit, click the is complete box and then click update
  8. You can repeat this process for tasks either for your group, or if you click "My Chores" you can have a seperate set of tasks for yourself
